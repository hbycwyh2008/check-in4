# Video Prototype Script

## Purpose

This is a very short English script for the video prototype of the final AP CSA FRQ feedback redesign. You can record your screen while clicking through the live demo and read this script naturally. It does not need to be read word-for-word. The goal is simply to give participants a quick overview before they complete the survey.

Live demo:

`https://hbycwyh2008.github.io/check-in4/`

## Recording Tips

- Open only the prototype in your browser
- Zoom in if the text looks too small
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Click through the prototype in the same order as the script
- Keep the total video around 1 minute

## Script

Hello, this video demonstrates my final prototype for redesigning AP Classroom feedback for AP Computer Science A free-response questions.

The goal of this design is to help students see where they lost points, understand why they lost points, and know what to practice next.

This first screen shows the overall result. The next screen breaks the feedback into rubric items so students can see where points were missed. Then the prototype gives more specific evidence and explanation connected to the student response. After that, it moves into practice guidance and a short next-step improvement flow.

Overall, this prototype tries to make feedback more useful as a learning tool, not just a score report.

Thank you for watching this video walkthrough of my final prototype.
