# Final Prototype Evaluation Survey

## AP Feedback Prototype Study

Thank you for participating in this study.

This survey is about a redesigned feedback page using AP Computer Science A free-response questions (FRQs) as the example case. Please first watch the video prototype or explore the live demo, then answer the questions below based on your honest impressions.

Estimated time: 5-8 minutes

There are no right or wrong answers.

## Section 1: Background

1. What is your experience with AP courses?

   - I have taken AP courses before
   - I have little or no AP course experience

2. How confident are you in understanding FRQ feedback pages in general?

- 1 = Not confident at all
